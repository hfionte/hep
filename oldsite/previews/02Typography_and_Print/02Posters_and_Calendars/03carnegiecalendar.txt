title = "Event Calendar"class = "Typography I,<br/>fall 2006"discussion = "<p>This calendar is designed to be folded across the middle and mailed to patrons of Carnegie Hall. The assignment allowed for only one typeface. The use of placement, size, and style allows the reader to easily identify different types of information. The placement above or below the date numbers creates a distinction between programs sponsored by Carnegie Hall or brought in by outside organizations.</p>"
